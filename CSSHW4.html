Akhil

Men's dressing can range from traditional attire like a kurta and pyjama to modern western wear, with choices influenced by occasion, comfort, and cultural context. A simple, breathable cotton kurta is suitable for casual daily wear, while a more formal, embellished Sherwani or Bandhgala is a regal choice for events like weddings. Fabric choice is key; cotton and linen are good for hot climates, while silk and rich brocades are ideal for cooler weather or grander occasions.
